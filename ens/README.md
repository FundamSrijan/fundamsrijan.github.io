# Expression & Stuff
This tool is made to automate the process of editing your custom minecraft bedrock skins to make them work with the expressions feature of the actions and stuff resource pack .

###### A Quick Tutorial
- [Open](https://fundamsrijan.github.io/ens) the tool .
- Upload your skin . You will get a quick look at your skin's face .
- Select if you want same eyes on both sides or different .
- Use the sliders and select the correct eye types .
- Select if you want the surprise expression .
- Configure eyebrows a similar way .
- Select if you want to clean glitching/interfereing pixels or not (Cleanup is recommended) .
- Select skin color . It will automatically be shaded for the eyelid .
- Click on download and enjoy !!!

###### Tips for Better Usage
- Your can try tapping on the eye and eyebrow previews for changing the background color and get a better visibility .
- When using surprise expression , make sure the eyes cover 1 extra pixel above them (2 for HD skins) . These will be normally be hidden under the eyebrows so make sure you select the correct eyebrows too .
- Clicking more colors will show a grid of all colors from your skin's face .
- [Video Tutorial](https://youtu.be/QDia3e12czc)

###### Some Notes
- Website is optimized for mobile devices (portrait mode) .
- HD skins are supported though actions and stuff itself doesn't support it .
- Cyclop eyes are not yet supported because they require a drawable system for eyeballs .
- Code is not well commented yet , so read at your own risk .
