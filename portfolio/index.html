HOW WE BRING YOUR VISION TO LIFE

NOTE : To ensure a smooth collaboration for both parties , I follow a standard professional workflow .

[1] DISCOVERY PHASE

The Start a project button leaves you to a google form where you can enter the necessary details about your project and preferred method of contact .
This helps me envision the exact goal of the project and understand your needs to deliver the best possible results to you .

[2] CONFIRMATION PHASE

Once you fill out the form , I'll reach you out in 24hrs through your selected means (or you can reach me out too) .
Then I'll tell you the delivery time and charge of the project . These heavily depend on the size , complexity and scope of the project .
To confirm the project , we then move on to the kickoff phase .

[3] KICKOFF PHASE

After that , to secure a slot in my production schedule and get your project marked in my calender , an initial commencement fee of 25% is required . After that is handled , the strategy phase is started .

[4] STRATEGY PHASE

In this phase , I'll take the required assets from you , discuss a little more about your project and strategize to work on it .

[5] DESIGN PHASE

In this phase , I start working on the project and send you an inital draft as a Proof of Concept . Here you can ask for major deisgn/layout/concept changes .

[6] ITERATION PHASE

I then iterate over your project again and again according to your needs . The more you tell me to iterate initially , the less revisions it will require later ultimately saving time and money .

[7] REFINEMENT PHASE

After designing , iterating and adding final touches the project , a draft optimized for preview will be provided to you . My goal is to ensure that the final product matches your vision , so I include 2-3 round of revisions .
NOTE : Extra revisions may charge you extra fees .
NOTE : Major design/layout/concept changes will not considered in reivsions . Demanding for those will be marked and charge as new project .

[8] FINAL DELIVERY & HAND-OFF

Upon approval of the preview draft and reciept of the remaining balance , I release the final files of the project .
